Dodo

The dodo is an extinct flightless bird that was endemic to the island of Mauritius,which is east of Madagascar in the Indian Ocean

Saber toothed Cats

These mammals are named for their long,swordlike canine teeth.All of the saber-toothed cats are distantly related to modern cats.

Woolly Rhinoceros

Woolly Rhinoceros was a prehistoric, thick-furred rhino that lived in Ice Age Europe and Asia.

A giant, gentle marine mammal that lived in the North Pacific and went extinct in the 18th century due to overhunting.

Auroch
